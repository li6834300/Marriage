About Us

Our Story

We have been together for 8 years. It all start with that conversation, that school, that mountain, and that cave. After graduate from Nanjing, we stayed in that city. Accompanied with us was that lovely 2br apartment, our first home. Then in New York, we are chasing our dream here, making our dream here, and creating our future here. We decided, no matter what happens, we will stay together forever!

Zhien - Groom, Love Home, Love Outdoor

I don't believe the first sight love. It took me a while to realise, oh I think I like this girl. Then I decided to chase her. This is a long process... I still remember it was July 15, 2007, she said 'Yes!' I felt that was the best time I ever had. I was on the train and reading the message. I, Zhien, finally had a girlfriend, that was my first thought. I felt the air arround me was different, it was full of sweetness. After that was our love in school, and work hard for staying in the same city, and struggle for getting to the US together. Qiqi was my power of doing everything. I will work hard to build a home for us and stay forever together.

Qijing - Bride, Love My Bed, Love my Zhizhi

I never thought I would get married in New York. Unconciously, 8 years passed. We stay together, encourage each other, and give hands to each other. I didn't think it clear until he said let's grow up together. It shocked me. In my heart, it was a vow, that means no matter what mistakes we would make, we can figure it out together. I'm not alone. From then on, where you go, I go. Nanjing, New York. When you go, I go. 2010, 2013. If I can't, I will wait, half a year or two and a half years. We made it, and grow up together. Thank you for being here with me, and sharing our experiences. Please give us your wishes, we also wish you guys best luck from now on.
